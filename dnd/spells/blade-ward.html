Blade Ward

Spell Details

Level: 0

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Materials:

Duration: 1 minute

Concentration: Yes

Description

Whenever a creature makes an attack roll against you before the spell ends, the attacker subtracts 1d4 from the attack roll.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
