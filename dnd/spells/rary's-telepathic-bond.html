Rary's Telepathic Bond

Spell Details

Level: 5

School: Divination

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: Yes

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S, M

Materials: (two eggs)

Duration: 1 hour

Concentration: No

Description

You forge a telepathic link among up to eight willing creatures of your choice within range, psychically linking each creature to all the others for the duration. Creatures that can't communicate in any languages aren't affected by this spell.

Until the psell ends, the targets can communicate telepathically through the bond whether or not they share a language. The communication is possible over any distance, though it an't extend to other planes of existence.

Classes: Bard, Wizard

Source: Player's Handbook 2024 pg. 311
