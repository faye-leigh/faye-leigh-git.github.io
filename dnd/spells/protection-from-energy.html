Protection from Energy

Spell Details

Level: 3

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Materials:

Duration: 1 hour

Concentration: Yes

Description

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has Resistance to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.

Classes: Cleric, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
