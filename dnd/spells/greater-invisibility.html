Greater Invisibility

Spell Details

Level: 4

School: Illusion

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Materials:

Duration: 1 minute

Concentration: Yes

Description

A creature you touch has the Invisible condition until the spell ends.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
