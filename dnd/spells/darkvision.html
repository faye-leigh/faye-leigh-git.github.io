Darkvision

Spell Details

Level: 2

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Touch

Components: V, S,M

Materials: (a dried carrot)

Duration: 8 hours

Concentration: No

Description

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has Darkvision with a range of 150 feet.

Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
