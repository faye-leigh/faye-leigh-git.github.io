Befuddlement

Spell Details

Level: 8

School: Enchantment

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: 150 feet

Components: V, S, M

Materials: (a key ring with no keys)

Duration: Instantaneous

Concentration: No

Description

You blast the mind of a creature that you can see within range. The target makes an Intelligence saving throw.

On a failed save, the target takes 10d12 Psychic damage and can't cast spells or take the Magic action. At the end of every 30 days the target repeats the save, ending the effect on a success. The effect can also be ended by the Greater Restoration, Heal, or Wish spells.

On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage only.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Warlock, Wizard

Source: Player's Handbook 2024 pg. 245
