Swift Quiver

Spell Details

Level: 5

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: Bonus Action

Ritual: No

Range: Self

Components: V, S, M

Materials: (a Quiver worth 1+ GP)

Duration: 1 minute

Concentration: Yes

Description

When you cast the spell and as a Bonus Action until it ends, you can make two attacks with a weapon that fires Arrows or Bolts, such as a Longbow or a Light Crossbow. the spell magially creates the ammunition needed for each attack. Each Arrow or Bolt created by the spell deals damage like a non-magical piece of ammunition of its kind and disintegrates immediately after it hits or misses.

Classes: Ranger

Source: Player's Handbook 2024 pg. 329
