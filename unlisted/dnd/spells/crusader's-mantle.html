Crusader's Mantle

Spell Details

Level: 3

School: Evocation

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Self

Components: V

Materials:

Duration: 1 minute

Concentration: Yes

Description

You radiate a magical aura in a 30-foot Emanation. While in the aura, you and your allies each deal an extra 1d4 Radiant damage when hitting with a weapon or an Unarmed Strike.

Classes: Paladin
