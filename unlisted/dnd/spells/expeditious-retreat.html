Expeditious Retreat

Spell Details

Level: 1

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: Bonus Action

Ritual: No

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Materials:

Duration: 10 minutes

Concentration: Yes

Description

You take the Dash action, and until the spell ends, you can take that action again as a Bonus Action.

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
