Shield of Faith

Spell Details

Level: 1

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: Bonus Action

Ritual: No

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S,M

Materials: (a prayer scroll)

Duration: 10 minutes

Concentration: Yes

Description

A shimmering field surrounds a creature of your choice within range, granting it a +2 bonus to AC for the duration.

Classes: Cleric, Paladin
