True Seeing

Spell Details

Level: 6

School: Divination

Casting Time: Action

Ritual: No

Range: Touch

Components: V, S,M

Materials: (mushroom powder worth 25+ GP, which the spell consumees)

Duration: 1 hour

Concentration: No

Description

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has Truesight with a range of 120 feet.

Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
